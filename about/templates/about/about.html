About KOR CBD

Keep On Rolling

KOR started as a company to provide medicinal CBD products to martial artists with the simple aim - Keep On Rolling. With many years of martial arts experience and competing internationally, we know the strain that martial can take on your body, and the long term impacts of this if it's not taken care of. Our CBD products have been designed to get martial artists back on the mats as soon as possible, and for as many years as possible.

Our CBD Products

Building on significant scientific research aroudn the power of CBD in treating and preventing athletic injudies, our range of products was born. We were concerned with the quality of some CBD products on the market, so we ensure that all our CBD products have 3rd party certification. This certification verifies the product quality and ensures that they do not contain illegal or controlled substances, something we know is really important to athletes who are out competing.

KOR CBD Customers

Starting out, our customers were almost exclusively martial arts athletes. A lot of them were competing at a high level, and many were finding conventional pharmaceutical treatments inaffective and inconvenient over the long term. The word has spread, and there are now athletes from a wide range of sports using our products. Judo, Tennis, Football, Hiking, Golf... You name it. If there's one thing that unites us across all sports, it's those aches and pains. We're really proud that KOR CBD is providing products that help keep so many athletes active.
